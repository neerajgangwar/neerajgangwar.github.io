    Masters Thesis

    Topic : Sparse Representation for Face Recognition
    Details
    This thesis focuses on the idea that many signals can be represented using very few coefficients with respect to certain bases. The main focus is on discriminative dictionary learning and its applications in the field of pattern classification.
    Thesis is available here and the final presentation is available here. All the codes used for simulations are available on GitHub.
